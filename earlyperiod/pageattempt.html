ABOUT ME

I’m a girl who’s been struggling with my weight for quite a while now and I made this blog to help and inspire me in my weight loss journey. I hope it inspires you too!

I'm 5'2 and 122 lbs (September 2016). The heaviest I’ve ever been was 152lbs.

I have scoliosis and ligamentous laxity, I used to do ballet and rithmic gymnastics but I had to stop due to those conditions.

I was also prediabetic and started a low glycemic index diet, along with some supplements, prescribed to me by an endocrinologist/nutricionist.

It has changed my life, and despite having somewhat of a downfall after moving in with my fiancee and us getting lazy and binge eating a lot, I still have been healthier these past couple of years more than any other time in the past 6 years, and now I'm losing what I've gained in the past months and so is my fiancee.

My ask box is opened if you ever wanna talk, ask me something or just say hi.
